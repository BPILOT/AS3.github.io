About Dunmore Hard Goods

Established in the heart of Sydney in 2008, Dunmore Hard Goods has swiftly cemented its status as the city's go-to hub for skateboard enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned pro, a weekend cruiser, or just starting your journey, our team at Dunmore has your back. Our shop is deeply rooted in the essence of Sydney's vibrant skate culture. We take pride in sourcing the best gear, both locally and globally. From custom decks adorned with exquisite artwork to premium trucks, wheels, and apparel, Dunmore's selection is curated with passion and precision.

The Difference We Bring

What sets us apart is our commitment to the community. We don't just sell products; we're a nexus for the skate community in Sydney. Hosting events, skate jams, and workshops, Dunmore is a melting pot for creativity, camaraderie, and, of course, some serious skate action. Venture into our store and let our knowledgeable staff guide you, or simply hang out, soak in the ambiance, and share a skate story or two. We are more than a shop; we are Dunmore Hard Goods - Sydney's skateboarding heartbeat.
